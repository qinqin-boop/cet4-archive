写作 Part(站长原创题目)

题目:Write a short essay (120-180 words) on "Why Volunteer Work Matters to Young People". Include:

  1. What volunteering teaches that classrooms cannot.
  2. One concrete example from your campus or city.
  3. How to start volunteering without overcommitting.
📖 点击查看答案 / 解析

范文骨架 — (1) 课堂教知识,志愿服务教共情(empathy)、组织能力、跨群体沟通;(2) 例子:校内的"老年人手机课"项目 — 学生每周末教社区老人用微信视频通话;(3) 起步建议:从每月一次的小活动开始,选择跟自己专业相关的项目以最大化双向收获。

大学生类话题作文一定要给具体、可信的例子(避免空喊口号)。结构上"问题 → 例子 → 解决路径"是 CET-4 高分套路。

翻译 Part(原创)

题目:请将下列中文译成英文(~100 字)。

故宫位于北京市中心,曾是中国明清两代二十四位皇帝的居所。它始建于一四〇六年,占地超过七十二万平方米,共有近九千间房屋,是世界上现存规模最大、保存最完整的木结构古建筑群。一九八七年,故宫被联合国教科文组织列入世界文化遗产名录。今天,它作为故宫博物院对外开放,每年接待来自世界各地的数千万游客。
📖 点击查看答案 / 解析

The Forbidden City, located in the heart of Beijing, was once the residence of twenty-four emperors of the Ming and Qing dynasties. Construction began in 1406. Covering an area of more than 720,000 square meters with nearly 9,000 rooms, it is the largest and best-preserved ancient wooden palace complex in the world. In 1987, the Forbidden City was inscribed on UNESCO's World Heritage List. Today, opened to the public as the Palace Museum, it welcomes tens of millions of visitors from around the world each year.

大数字英文表达 — 720,000 / 9,000;朝代名 Ming / Qing dynasties;UNESCO 大写;名称 The Forbidden City + The Palace Museum 两个并行。

阅读 Part(原创短文)

Passage(原创,~200 词,主题碳中和):

Carbon neutrality — the goal of removing as much CO₂ from the atmosphere as one emits — has become the central commitment of national climate policy in dozens of countries. Meeting this target requires more than installing solar panels. It demands a comprehensive overhaul of how electricity is generated, how buildings are heated, and how goods move across borders.

>

Renewable energy now accounts for roughly thirty percent of new power capacity worldwide. Yet the intermittency of wind and sunlight forces grids to invest heavily in storage technologies, ranging from lithium batteries to hydrogen tanks. Industries hard to electrify — cement, steel, long-haul aviation — remain particularly stubborn challenges.

>

Beyond technology, behavioral change is equally important. Compact cities with walkable neighborhoods can cut transport emissions sharply. Diets lower in beef and dairy reduce agricultural methane. Even modest adjustments by hundreds of millions of urban residents add up to gigatons of avoided emissions.

Question 1: According to the passage, which of the following is NOT required for carbon neutrality?

A. Renewable electricity B. Compact, walkable cities C. Hard-to-electrify industries staying unchanged D. Storage technologies

📖 点击查看答案 / 解析

C

文章把 cement / steel / aviation 列为 "stubborn challenges" 即必须改变,选 C 反向命中。
