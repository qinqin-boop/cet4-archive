## 原创仿真模拟卷

### 写作 Part

::ex
**题目**:Write an essay (120-180 words) on *"The Influence of Short Videos on College Students"*. Include:
1. Why short videos attract students.
2. Possible negative effects on study and concentration.
3. How students can use short videos responsibly.

**答案**:范文要点 — (1) 短视频满足即时娱乐与信息获取需求,算法精准推送让人难以抽离;(2) 影响:碎片化注意力,挤压深度学习时间;(3) 建议:设定每日使用上限,把短视频作为放松工具而非主食。结尾再点题。

**解析**:议论文三段式,务必给出**具体例子**或数据(可虚构合理范围,如"some studies show 60% of college students spend over 2 hours daily on short-video apps")。
::end

### 翻译 Part(原创)

::ex
**题目**:请将下列中文译成英文(~100 字)。

> 中国剪纸是一种源远流长的民间艺术。剪纸艺人用一把剪刀在红色或彩色的纸上剪出花鸟鱼虫、神话人物、传统节日图案等。逢年过节,人们把剪纸贴在窗户、门楣或灯笼上,寓意吉祥如意、幸福平安。如今,剪纸不仅出现在传统节日中,还作为礼品和装饰走向世界,成为讲述中国故事的一种独特艺术语言。

**答案**:*Chinese paper-cutting is a traditional folk art with a long history. Using a single pair of scissors, artists cut intricate patterns of flowers, birds, fish, mythical figures, and festive motifs out of red or colored paper. During festivals, paper-cuts are pasted on windows, door lintels, or lanterns to symbolize good fortune, happiness, and peace. Today, paper-cutting appears not only in traditional celebrations but also as gifts and decorations around the world, becoming a unique artistic language for telling the story of China.*

**解析**:文化词翻译策略 — "剪纸 paper-cutting / 红纸 red paper / 窗户 windows / 门楣 door lintels / 灯笼 lanterns / 吉祥如意 good fortune / 幸福平安 happiness and peace"。句末"讲述中国故事"用 "telling the story of China",符合英文表达习惯。
::end

### 阅读 Part(原创短文)

::ex
**Passage**(原创,~200 词):

> The shift to remote work, accelerated by the pandemic, is reshaping cities. In many countries, downtown office vacancy rates have reached historic highs while suburban housing prices have surged. Workers freed from daily commuting are choosing larger homes farther from city centers, prioritizing space and natural surroundings over proximity to the workplace.
>
> This trend produces mixed effects. On one hand, remote work improves quality of life for many: fewer hours wasted in traffic, more time with family, and the freedom to live in regions with lower costs. On the other, downtown businesses — coffee shops, dry cleaners, sandwich bars — that once depended on office foot traffic are struggling. Some cities have begun converting empty office buildings into residential apartments to revive their cores.
>
> Researchers note that hybrid work arrangements, in which employees work from home two or three days a week, may become the new norm. Companies are investing in collaboration tools to keep distributed teams productive, while urban planners are rethinking how downtown areas can serve residents rather than just commuters.

**Question 1**: According to the passage, why are suburban housing prices rising?

A. Suburbs offer better schools.
B. People prefer space and nature when commuting is no longer required.
C. Cities have banned new housing construction.
D. Companies are moving headquarters to suburbs.

**答案**:B

**解析**:第一段明确"prioritizing space and natural surroundings over proximity to the workplace"。
::end

::ex
**Question 2**: Who is most negatively affected by the shift to remote work?

A. Software engineers
B. Downtown small businesses
C. Urban planners
D. Suburban homeowners

**答案**:B

**解析**:第二段直接列出 coffee shops / dry cleaners / sandwich bars are struggling。
::end

## 备考建议

::tip
- 听力训练时注意"原因-结果"结构(because / so / therefore)
- 翻译加强**文化词清单背诵**(瓷器 porcelain / 丝绸 silk / 剪纸 paper-cutting)
- 阅读 passage 类型:议论文 + 说明文为主,每段开头是 topic sentence,先扫一遍
::end
