## 原创仿真模拟卷

### 写作 Part(站长原创题目)

::ex
**题目**:Write a short essay (120-180 words) on the topic *"The Role of Reading in University Life"*. You should include:
1. Why reading matters to college students.
2. What kinds of books a student should read.
3. How to develop a daily reading habit.

**答案**:参考范文 — *In an era flooded with short videos and instant entertainment, reading remains an irreplaceable habit for university students. First, books cultivate deep thinking, training students to follow long, structured arguments rather than scattered snippets of information. Second, beyond textbooks, students should explore biographies, popular science, and classic novels to broaden their worldview. Finally, a daily reading habit can be built by allocating just 30 minutes before sleep and keeping a simple notebook of impressions. In short, reading is not a luxury but the quietest, most cost-effective investment a student can make in themselves.* (约 110 词)

**解析**:CET-4 写作三段式套路:(1) 现象引入+主题句,(2) 3 个分论点细化,(3) 结论收束。注意时态(一般现在时为主)、过渡词(First / Second / Finally / In short)、连贯性。
::end

### 听力 Part(原创对话脚本+练习)

::ex
**Conversation 1**(原创短对话脚本):
W: Did you finish the reading list for tomorrow's seminar?
M: Honestly, I only got through half. The third article was so technical that I had to look up every other word.
W: I had the same problem. Maybe we should form a study group to share notes.
M: Great idea. Let's invite Lisa and Tom too.

**Question**: What does the man suggest at the end?

**答案**:He agrees to form a study group and adds Lisa and Tom.

**解析**:听力短对话 typically 第二个人的最后一句话是答案锚点(标志词 "Great idea / Let's / Why don't we" 等表态)。听完整个对话再选,不要听到关键词就选。
::end

::ex
**Conversation 2**(原创):
M: I'm thinking of switching my major from accounting to computer science. What do you think?
W: Have you considered the workload? CS courses involve a lot of late-night coding.
M: I know it'll be harder, but accounting feels too repetitive for me.
W: Then you should talk to the academic advisor first before making the final decision.

**Question**: What does the woman advise the man to do?

**答案**:Consult the academic advisor before deciding.

**解析**:听力题"建议类"题目锁定 "should" / "had better" / "why don't you" 句型。
::end

### 阅读理解 Part(原创短文+5 道题)

::ex
**Passage**(原创短文,~280 词,可读性接近 CET-4 难度):

> **Why Sleep Quality Beats Sleep Quantity**
>
> For decades, the popular advice has been "get eight hours of sleep every night". Recent research, however, suggests that the *quality* of sleep matters at least as much as its length. Two people sleeping the same number of hours may wake up with very different levels of alertness depending on how much deep sleep and REM sleep they actually completed.
>
> Deep sleep, the stage when the body repairs tissue and consolidates memories, occupies roughly twenty percent of a healthy sleep cycle. REM sleep, characterized by rapid eye movement, is when the brain processes emotions and creative connections. A sleep cycle disturbed by noise, alcohol, or late-night screen exposure tends to lose deep sleep first, leaving the sleeper feeling foggy even after eight or nine hours in bed.
>
> Improving sleep quality is often easier than extending sleep duration. Sleep researchers recommend keeping the bedroom temperature between 18 and 20 °C, avoiding caffeine after noon, and dimming screens at least one hour before bed. A simple bedtime routine — reading a paper book, light stretching, or meditation — signals to the body that the day is winding down.
>
> University students, who often face irregular schedules, can benefit most from prioritizing sleep quality. Pulling an all-nighter before an exam typically backfires, as the brain consolidates new knowledge precisely during the sleep one sacrifices. A consistent seven hours of deep, undisturbed sleep is now widely considered more effective than nine restless hours.

**Question 1**: According to the passage, what determines morning alertness?

A. The total number of hours slept
B. The quality of sleep cycles (deep + REM)
C. The age of the sleeper
D. Whether one took a nap during the day

**答案**:B

**解析**:文章开头明确"the quality of sleep matters at least as much as its length",并提到深睡和 REM 睡眠的不同作用。
::end

::ex
**Question 2**: Deep sleep is associated with all of the following EXCEPT:

A. Tissue repair
B. Memory consolidation
C. Rapid eye movement
D. About 20% of a sleep cycle

**答案**:C

**解析**:Rapid eye movement 属于 REM 阶段,不是 deep sleep。这是细节排除题,注意 EXCEPT 三个字必须圈出。
::end

::ex
**Question 3**: What is the author's suggestion for improving sleep quality?(选最佳)

A. Sleep nine hours every night.
B. Keep the bedroom temperature around 18-20°C and avoid afternoon caffeine.
C. Sleep with the light on.
D. Pull all-nighters before exams.

**答案**:B

**解析**:第三段明确列出 18-20°C 与下午后避免咖啡因。其它选项 A 强调时长(被文章反驳)、C 与"dim screens"相反、D 被最后一段明确反对。
::end

::ex
**Question 4**(主旨题):What is the main idea of this passage?

A. Eight hours of sleep is necessary for everyone.
B. Sleep quality matters as much as or more than sleep quantity.
C. University students should never sleep less than nine hours.
D. REM sleep is the only important part of sleep.

**答案**:B

**解析**:主旨题选最 general 的句子,即开头第二句的核心思想。A 与文章主旨相反;C 是过度推论;D 忽视了 deep sleep 的重要性。
::end

::ex
**Question 5**(推断题):What can be inferred about university students?

A. They never need to study late.
B. Their sleep schedules are often irregular.
C. They sleep more than working adults.
D. They are immune to sleep deprivation.

**答案**:B

**解析**:第四段第一句 "often face irregular schedules" 是直接证据。其他选项过度推论或反向。
::end

### 翻译 Part(原创中文段落,中译英)

::ex
**题目**(原创):请将下列中文段落译成英文(~110 字)。

> 中国的二十四节气是一种古老的时间体系,它把一年划分为二十四个等长的部分,每个部分大约十五天。每个节气都对应着特定的农业活动和饮食习惯。例如,"立春"标志着春天的开始,人们会吃春饼以庆祝;"冬至"则是一年中白天最短的一天,北方家庭习惯包饺子,南方人则吃汤圆。二零一六年,二十四节气被联合国教科文组织列入人类非物质文化遗产名录,成为中华传统文化向世界展示的一张名片。

**答案**:参考译文 — *China's Twenty-Four Solar Terms is an ancient time-keeping system that divides a year into twenty-four equal parts, each lasting approximately fifteen days. Each solar term corresponds to specific agricultural activities and dietary customs. For example, Lichun marks the beginning of spring, when people eat spring pancakes to celebrate; the Winter Solstice, the shortest day of the year, is traditionally celebrated by northern families wrapping dumplings while southerners eat sweet glutinous rice balls. In 2016, the Twenty-Four Solar Terms was inscribed on UNESCO's Representative List of the Intangible Cultural Heritage of Humanity, becoming a cultural calling card showcasing Chinese tradition to the world.*

**解析**:CET-4 翻译注意四点:
1. **专有名词处理**:节气名 Lichun / Winter Solstice 保持拼音 + 释义,UNESCO 大写;
2. **时态**:历史 / 长期事实用一般现在时,2016 年事件用一般过去时;
3. **句式简化**:中文长句可拆 2-3 个英文短句,不要硬翻;
4. **文化词解释**:春饼 spring pancakes、饺子 dumplings、汤圆 sweet glutinous rice balls — 用 dumplings 通用名 + 描述。
::end
