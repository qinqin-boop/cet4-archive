Section A: 短对话(Short Conversations)

题 1
W: Have you decided which elective course to take next semester? M: I'm torn between Modern Art History and Introduction to Data Science. W: Why not pick the one that helps your future career? M: That makes sense. I'll go with data science then.

Q: Which course will the man choose?

A. Modern Art History   B. Introduction to Data Science   C. Neither   D. Both

📖 查看答案 / 解析

答案: B

解析: 女士建议选对职业有帮助的,男士接受"That makes sense. I'll go with data science",明确选 B。

题 2
M: I noticed you've been jogging every morning recently. W: Yes, I started a fitness plan three weeks ago. I want to run a half-marathon in October. M: That's impressive. Mind if I join you tomorrow morning? W: Not at all. Meet me at the park gate at six.

Q: What will the two speakers do tomorrow morning?

A. Watch a marathon   B. Jog together   C. Have breakfast   D. Visit a park

📖 查看答案 / 解析

答案: B

解析: 男士问"Mind if I join you",女士同意"Meet me at the park gate at six",一起跑步。注意 B 是 "jog together" 而不是 D "visit a park"(地点是细节,不是行为)。

题 3
W: The library will be renovated starting next month. Where are we supposed to study? M: I heard the old gymnasium will be temporarily converted into a study area. W: Really? That's a long walk from the dorm. M: They'll run a shuttle bus every twenty minutes.

Q: How will students travel to the temporary study area?

A. On foot   B. By bicycle   C. By shuttle bus   D. By taxi

📖 查看答案 / 解析

答案: C

解析: 男士最后一句明确"They'll run a shuttle bus every twenty minutes"。陷阱选项 A 来自女士抱怨 long walk,实际有班车不需步行。

题 4
M: My laptop has been running really slow lately. I'm thinking of buying a new one. W: Have you tried clearing the cache and uninstalling unused apps first? M: Not yet. Do you think that will make a real difference? W: It often does. Save your money before you buy anything new.

Q: What does the woman suggest the man do?

A. Buy a new laptop   B. Try maintenance first   C. Borrow her laptop   D. Visit a repair shop

📖 查看答案 / 解析

答案: B

解析: "Have you tried clearing the cache..." 是建议先维护;"Save your money before you buy anything new" 进一步明确不要急着换新机。

题 5
W: I'm submitting my thesis on Friday. Could you read the introduction tonight? M: Sorry, I have an exam tomorrow morning and need to focus. How about Thursday afternoon? W: That cuts it close, but it should work. M: Email it over and I'll give you detailed notes.

Q: When will the man read the woman's introduction?

A. Tonight   B. Tomorrow morning   C. Thursday afternoon   D. Friday

📖 查看答案 / 解析

答案: C

解析: 男士有 exam 不能今晚,主动提"How about Thursday afternoon",女士同意。

Section B: 长对话(Long Conversation)

题 6 - 8
M: I heard you spent your summer interning at a non-profit organization. How was it? W: It was eye-opening. I worked on a community gardening project in low-income neighborhoods. M: Did you find it meaningful? W: Definitely. I learned a lot more about urban poverty than any textbook taught me. We helped families grow vegetables on rooftops and small lots. M: Sounds rewarding. Did the experience change your career plans? W: Actually, yes. I came in thinking I wanted to be an investment banker, but now I'm seriously considering public policy. M: That's a big shift. Were the working hours intense? W: Surprisingly manageable. Around forty hours a week, no late nights. The pay was modest but the experience was priceless.

Q6: What did the woman do during the summer?

A. Worked at an investment bank   B. Interned at a non-profit   C. Traveled abroad   D. Took summer courses

📖 查看答案 / 解析

答案: B

解析: 男士开场直接问 "spent your summer interning at a non-profit",女士确认。

Q7: What career change is the woman now considering?

A. From banker to policy work   B. From teacher to banker   C. From engineer to policy   D. From doctor to teacher

📖 查看答案 / 解析

答案: A

解析: "I came in thinking I wanted to be an investment banker, but now... public policy"。

Q8: What does the woman say about the working hours?

A. Excessively long   B. Around 40 hours per week, manageable   C. Less than 20 hours   D. Flexible but stressful

📖 查看答案 / 解析

答案: B

解析: "Around forty hours a week, no late nights" 直接命中。

Section C: 短文听写(Passage)

题 9 - 10
Many universities have begun offering short, intensive courses during winter and summer breaks. These mini-terms typically last two to four weeks and focus on a single topic, allowing students to earn three or four credits in a compressed format. Supporters argue that the immersive format helps students absorb material faster, since they are not juggling multiple subjects at once. Critics, however, point out that deep learning requires time for ideas to settle, and that cramming a semester's worth of content into a few weeks can leave students with shallow understanding. Whatever the debate, enrollment in mini-terms has grown rapidly, with many students using the format to graduate earlier or to retake a difficult course in a focused setting.

Q9: What is the typical length of a mini-term?

A. One week   B. Two to four weeks   C. Six weeks   D. A full semester

📖 查看答案 / 解析

答案: B

解析: "typically last two to four weeks" 直接命中。

Q10: What is the critics' main concern about mini-terms?

A. They are too expensive   B. They produce shallow understanding   C. They reduce graduation rates   D. Teachers refuse to teach them

📖 查看答案 / 解析

答案: B

解析: "cramming a semester's worth of content into a few weeks can leave students with shallow understanding" 直接命中。
