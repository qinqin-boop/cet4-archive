Section A · Listening

听力 题 1
W: The library will be renovated starting next month. Where are we supposed to study? M: I heard the old gymnasium will be temporarily converted into a study area. W: Really? That's a long walk from the dorm. M: They'll run a shuttle bus every twenty minutes.

Q: How will students travel to the temporary study area?

正确答案: C

解析: shuttle bus every twenty minutes。

听力 题 2
M: My laptop has been running really slow lately. I'm thinking of buying a new one. W: Have you tried clearing the cache and uninstalling unused apps first? M: Not yet. Do you think that will make a real difference? W: It often does. Save your money before you buy anything new.

Q: What does the woman suggest the man do?

正确答案: B

解析: Save your money before you buy anything new。

听力 题 3
M: My final exam in statistics overlaps with the literature paper deadline. I'm stressed. W: Have you talked to either professor? M: Not yet. I thought I should solve it myself. W: That's the wrong instinct. Professors usually offer extensions if you explain in advance, but not the night before.

Q: What is the woman's main point?

正确答案: B

解析: Professors offer extensions if you explain in advance。

听力 题 4
W: I'm writing my graduate school personal statement and it feels stiff. M: Show, don't tell. Instead of writing 'I am passionate about research', tell the moment a research result actually surprised you. W: That makes sense. Specific stories beat general adjectives. M: Exactly. Admissions officers read thousands of statements that say 'I am passionate'.

Q: What writing advice does the man give?

正确答案: B

解析: Show, don't tell + Specific stories beat general adjectives。

听力 题 5
M: I'm thinking of dropping my Tuesday morning class. W: Why? You signed up because the professor's research matches your interest. M: True, but I'm exhausted after staying up reading the assignment. W: The problem isn't the class, it's that you postpone the reading. Move it to Saturday.

Q: What does the woman identify as the real problem?

正确答案: B

解析: The problem isn't the class, it's that you postpone the reading。

Section B · Reading

Passage: Remote Work and Cities

The rapid spread of remote work has changed how cities function. Office vacancy rates in major financial districts have reached levels not seen in decades, while suburban housing markets have boomed. Workers who once commuted ninety minutes are buying larger homes in quieter areas. Coffee shops and dry cleaners that depended on office foot traffic are struggling. Hybrid work — two or three days in office — is settling in as a new norm. Researchers find fully remote workers report higher loneliness, while fully in-office workers report less flexibility. A balanced rhythm offers the best of both worlds.
阅读 题 1

What is happening to office vacancy rates?

正确答案: C

解析: 第二句明确。

阅读 题 2

Why are workers buying suburban homes?

正确答案: B

解析: larger homes in quieter areas。

阅读 题 3

Which businesses are struggling?

正确答案: B

解析: 直接列举。

阅读 题 4

What is hybrid work?

正确答案: C

解析: 原文明确。

Passage: The Future of Books

Some predicted that e-books would kill paper books, but the data tells a more interesting story. After early excitement, e-book sales plateaued and paper sales have actually grown in many markets. Readers report that paper books invite slower, more reflective reading, while e-readers excel for travel and instant access. The two formats coexist for different needs. Independent bookstores have rebounded by offering events, curated recommendations, and a community space that online retailers cannot replicate. Paper books, far from dying, have found a renewed value as a quiet alternative to screens.
阅读 题 1

What did some people predict?

正确答案: B

解析: predicted that e-books would kill paper。

阅读 题 2

What does the data actually show?

正确答案: B

解析: After early excitement, e-book sales plateaued。

阅读 题 3

Why have independent bookstores rebounded?

正确答案: B

解析: events, curated recommendations, and a community space。

阅读 题 4

What value of paper books does the author highlight?

正确答案: B

解析: a quiet alternative to screens。

Section C · Translation

翻译 题 1

请将下列中文译成英文:

近年来,中国在人工智能领域取得了显著进展。从智能语音助手到自动驾驶汽车,人工智能正在改变人们的生活和工作方式。

翻译 题 2

请将下列中文译成英文:

中医是中国传统医学,有数千年的历史。它包括针灸、推拿、中草药等多种治疗方法。中医强调人与自然的和谐。

参考译文: Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) is a system of medicine with thousands of years of history. It includes acupuncture, tuina massage, and herbal medicine. TCM emphasizes harmony between humans and nature.

解析: 针灸 acupuncture / 中草药 herbal medicine。

翻译 题 3

请将下列中文译成英文:

端午节是中国传统节日之一,通常在农历五月初五。人们吃粽子、赛龙舟,纪念古代爱国诗人屈原。这个节日已被列入联合国非物质文化遗产。

参考译文: The Dragon Boat Festival is a traditional Chinese holiday, usually celebrated on the fifth day of the fifth lunar month. People eat zongzi and race dragon boats to commemorate the ancient patriotic poet Qu Yuan. The festival has been listed by UNESCO as Intangible Cultural Heritage.

解析: 粽子 zongzi / 联合国非物质文化遗产 UNESCO Intangible Cultural Heritage。

Section D · Writing

写作 题 1

Write an essay (120-180 words) on "Balancing Study and Leisure in College Life".

参考范文: College life is short and students often fail to balance study and leisure. Too much study leads to burnout; too much leisure damages GPA. A practical strategy is to schedule one fixed non-work day per week for hobbies and to protect a few hours of deep-work time each weekday with notifications off. Real balance is a sustainable rhythm one can maintain for years.

解析: 平衡话题。

写作 题 2

Write an essay (120-180 words) on "Why Volunteer Work Matters to Young People".

参考范文: Volunteer work teaches young people what no classroom can. While lectures explain theories, volunteering puts students face to face with strangers whose lives differ from their own. From these encounters grow empathy and organizational skills. A simple example: a campus program where students teach elderly community members how to make video calls. Even one Saturday morning per month can shape character.

解析: 具体例子。
