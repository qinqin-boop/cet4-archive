题 1 · 文化

请将下列中文译成英文:

春节是中国最重要的传统节日,通常在公历一月底或二月初到来。家人无论身在何处都会回到家中,共享团圆饭、贴春联、放鞭炮。如今,虽然现代生活节奏加快,但走亲访友、给孩子压岁钱等习俗仍然代代相传。

📖 参考译文 / 解析

参考译文: The Spring Festival is the most important traditional Chinese holiday, typically falling in late January or early February of the Gregorian calendar. Wherever family members may be, they return home to share a reunion dinner, paste spring couplets on doors, and set off firecrackers. Although modern life has become faster paced, customs such as visiting relatives and giving children "lucky money" continue to be passed down from generation to generation.

解析: 文化词处理 — 春联 spring couplets / 压岁钱 lucky money (or hongbao) / 鞭炮 firecrackers。"无论身在何处" 用 wherever 引导让步状语。"代代相传" 用 passed down from generation to generation。

题 2 · 文化

请将下列中文译成英文:

中国书法是一种独特的视觉艺术,有三千多年的历史。它不仅是书写汉字的方式,也是表达情感、修养身心的途径。书法家通过笔锋的轻重、墨色的浓淡和留白的多少,创造出富有节奏感的作品。学习书法被认为有助于培养专注力和耐心。

📖 参考译文 / 解析

参考译文: Chinese calligraphy is a unique visual art with a history of more than three thousand years. It is not merely a way to write Chinese characters but also a means of expressing emotions and cultivating the body and mind. Through varying brush pressure, ink density, and the use of negative space, calligraphers create works with a strong sense of rhythm. Learning calligraphy is widely believed to nurture focus and patience.

解析: 专业词 — 笔锋 brush pressure / 墨色浓淡 ink density / 留白 negative space。"不仅...也" 用 not merely... but also。"被认为" 用 is widely believed。

题 3 · 科技

请将下列中文译成英文:

近年来,中国在人工智能领域取得了显著进展。从智能语音助手到自动驾驶汽车,人工智能技术正在改变人们的日常生活和工作方式。同时,政府高度重视人工智能伦理与安全问题,出台了一系列规范,确保这项技术的发展真正服务于社会。

📖 参考译文 / 解析

参考译文: In recent years, China has made remarkable progress in the field of artificial intelligence. From smart voice assistants to self-driving cars, AI technologies are transforming the way people live and work. At the same time, the government places great emphasis on issues of AI ethics and safety, issuing a series of regulations to ensure that the development of this technology truly serves society.

解析: "显著进展" remarkable progress;"改变...方式" transforming the way... ;"高度重视" places great emphasis on;"出台规范" issuing regulations。

题 4 · 文化

请将下列中文译成英文:

中医是中国传统医学,有数千年的历史。它包括针灸、推拿、中草药等多种治疗方法。中医强调人与自然的和谐,注重整体调理而非局部治疗。如今,越来越多的国外医疗机构也开始引入中医作为辅助疗法,为全球医疗体系做出独特贡献。

📖 参考译文 / 解析

参考译文: Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) is a system of medicine with a history of thousands of years. It includes therapies such as acupuncture, tuina massage, and herbal medicine. TCM emphasizes harmony between humans and nature, focusing on holistic adjustment rather than localized treatment. Today, an increasing number of foreign medical institutions are introducing TCM as a complementary therapy, making a unique contribution to the global healthcare system.

解析: 中医 TCM (Traditional Chinese Medicine);针灸 acupuncture;推拿 tuina massage (or therapeutic massage);中草药 herbal medicine;辅助疗法 complementary therapy。

题 5 · 教育

请将下列中文译成英文:

近年来,中国的高等教育发展迅速。越来越多的学生有机会进入大学学习,留学的人数也持续增长。与此同时,教育部门积极推动职业教育与本科教育并重的政策,鼓励学生根据自身兴趣和能力选择最适合的发展道路。

📖 参考译文 / 解析

参考译文: Higher education in China has developed rapidly in recent years. An increasing number of students now have the opportunity to attend university, and the number of those studying abroad continues to grow. Meanwhile, the Ministry of Education actively promotes a policy that gives equal weight to vocational and undergraduate education, encouraging students to choose the path of development that best fits their interests and abilities.

解析: "高等教育" higher education;"留学" study abroad;"职业教育" vocational education;"并重" equal weight to。

题 6 · 文化

请将下列中文译成英文:

丝绸之路是古代连接中国与中亚、西亚乃至欧洲的贸易通道,因中国丝绸沿此路输出而得名。它不仅是商品流通的道路,也是文化、宗教和技术交流的桥梁。如今,"一带一路"倡议在某种意义上是这一古老精神的现代延续。

📖 参考译文 / 解析

参考译文: The Silk Road was an ancient network of trade routes connecting China with Central Asia, West Asia, and even Europe. It took its name from the Chinese silk that flowed along it. More than a route for goods, it served as a bridge for cultural, religious, and technological exchange. Today, the Belt and Road Initiative can be seen, in a sense, as a modern continuation of this ancient spirit.

解析: 丝绸之路 The Silk Road;一带一路 The Belt and Road Initiative;"在某种意义上" in a sense。

题 7 · 经济

请将下列中文译成英文:

过去三十年,中国的城市化进程加快,数亿人从农村迁入城市。这种大规模的人口流动既带来了经济增长,也带来了住房、教育、医疗等方面的挑战。政府正在通过新型城镇化战略,推动城市更宜居,公共服务更均衡。

📖 参考译文 / 解析

参考译文: Over the past three decades, China's urbanization has accelerated, with hundreds of millions of people moving from rural areas to cities. This large-scale population movement has brought both economic growth and challenges in housing, education, and healthcare. The government is now advancing a new-style urbanization strategy to make cities more livable and public services more equitably distributed.

解析: 城市化 urbanization;新型城镇化 new-style urbanization;均衡 equitably distributed。

题 8 · 文化

请将下列中文译成英文:

中国的茶文化源远流长。无论是日常待客还是商务洽谈,泡一壶好茶都是表达尊重的方式。不同地区有不同的饮茶习惯:北方人喜欢花茶,江南人偏爱绿茶,福建广东则以乌龙茶闻名。一杯茶,既能解渴,也能拉近人与人之间的距离。

📖 参考译文 / 解析

参考译文: Chinese tea culture has a long history. Whether welcoming guests in daily life or negotiating in business settings, brewing a good pot of tea is a way to show respect. Different regions have different tea preferences: northerners enjoy scented tea, people in the Jiangnan area favor green tea, while Fujian and Guangdong are famous for oolong tea. A simple cup of tea can both quench thirst and bring people closer together.

解析: 花茶 scented tea (jasmine 是其中一种);乌龙茶 oolong tea;"拉近距离" bring people closer together。
